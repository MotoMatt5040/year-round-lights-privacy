Year-Round Lights — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 21, 2026

This policy explains what data the Year-Round Lights mobile app (the “App”) accesses, how it uses that data, and what — if anything — leaves your device. It also describes your privacy rights under United States state privacy laws.

1. Data we collect

The App does not collect personal data about you.

Specifically, the App does not collect or transmit:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or any contact information
  • Your geographic location (on Android the App requests Location permission only because the operating system requires it for Wi-Fi scanning, and on iOS only so the operating system will return your current Wi-Fi network name — see Section 3. In both cases, the App never reads, displays, transmits, or stores your geographic coordinates)
  • Photos, contacts, calendar entries, microphone or camera input
  • Browsing history or analytics about how you use the App
  • Any data classified as “sensitive personal information” under California or other state privacy laws (no Social Security numbers, driver's license numbers, account credentials, precise geolocation, racial or ethnic origin, religious beliefs, union membership, mail/email/text contents, genetic data, biometric identifiers, health, or sexual orientation/sex-life data)

The App does send anonymized crash reports to Sentry when an unexpected error occurs. These reports contain the technical stack trace of the error, the device model, the operating system version, and the App version — nothing more. You can review Sentry's privacy practices at the link above.

2. Data stored on your device

To configure your lighting controller, the App stores the following information on your device only, encrypted using the device's secure storage (the iOS Keychain on iPhone and iPad, the Android Keystore on Android):

  • The Wi-Fi network name (SSID) and password you provide during controller setup
  • The IP address and MAC address of each lighting controller you add
  • Your saved themes, schedules, and color presets

This information never leaves your device and is not transmitted to the developer, to Apple or Google, or to any third party. It is deleted when you uninstall the App.

3. Permissions we request

PermissionWhy the App requests it
Location (Fine / Coarse) — Android Required by Android in order to scan for Wi-Fi networks during initial controller setup. The App does not read or store your geographic location.
Location (When In Use) — iOS Required by iOS for the operating system to return your current Wi-Fi network name, which the App suggests when adding a new lighting controller. The App does not read or store your geographic coordinates.
Nearby Wi-Fi devices (Android 13+) Replacement for the Location permission on newer Android versions; same purpose — Wi-Fi scanning during setup.
Change Wi-Fi state Used briefly during initial setup to connect your phone to the controller's temporary “LEDnet…” network so the App can hand it your home Wi-Fi credentials.
Bluetooth (scan and connect) Used during initial controller setup. The App scans for nearby lighting controllers, connects to a controller you select via Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE), and sends your home Wi-Fi network name and password to that controller so it can join your Wi-Fi. The Wi-Fi credentials travel only between your phone and the controller you are setting up; they are never sent to the developer or to any third party.
Local Network — iOS Required by iOS to discover and send commands to your lighting controller over your home Wi-Fi after setup. The App communicates only with controllers on your local network and does not scan, identify, or transmit information about other devices.
Internet Used to send commands to the lighting controller on your local Wi-Fi, and (only when a Sentry crash-reporting key is present in the build) to deliver crash reports to Sentry over HTTPS.

5. We do not sell or share your personal information

The Service Provider does not sell or share your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising. No personal information is sold, traded, rented, or otherwise transferred to third parties for monetary or other valuable consideration. Because no such sale or sharing occurs, there is no “Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information” mechanism to provide.

6. Your rights as a US resident

Depending on the state in which you reside (including California, Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut, Utah, Texas, Oregon, Montana, Florida, and other states with comprehensive privacy laws), you may have the following rights with respect to your personal information:

  • Right to know / access: request confirmation of whether we process personal information about you, and a copy of that information.
  • Right to delete: request that we delete personal information we have collected from you.
  • Right to correct: request that we correct inaccurate personal information.
  • Right to portability: receive a copy of your personal information in a portable, readily usable format.
  • Right to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information, or of targeted advertising or profiling. (As stated in Section 5, the App does not engage in any of these activities.)
  • Right to limit use of sensitive personal information. (The App does not collect sensitive personal information.)
  • Right against discrimination: we will not deny you service, charge different prices, or provide a different level of quality because you exercised any of these rights.

How to exercise your rights

Email Dwheel79@gmail.com with the subject line “Privacy Request” and a description of which right you want to exercise. We will respond within 45 days as required by applicable state law (or sooner where required). Because the App does not collect personal data tied to your identity, in most cases your request can be honored simply by uninstalling the App, which deletes all locally-stored information. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling certain requests; verification is limited to confirming that you are the user making the request.

Authorized agents (California)

California residents may designate an authorized agent to make a request on their behalf. The agent must provide written authorization signed by you, and we may require you to verify the agent's authority directly.

Right to appeal

If we deny your request, residents of states whose laws grant a right of appeal (including Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut, and others) may appeal our decision by replying to our denial email within a reasonable time. We will respond within 60 days. If your appeal is denied, you may contact your state Attorney General.

8. Data retention

On-device data (Wi-Fi credentials, device list, themes, schedules) is retained for as long as the App is installed and is deleted automatically when you uninstall the App.

Crash reports sent to Sentry are retained according to Sentry's retention policy (typically 90 days for the free tier). We do not retain copies of crash reports outside of Sentry.

9. Children

The App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13 (consistent with the federal Children's Online Privacy Protection Act, COPPA). If you believe a child under 13 has used the App and you are their parent or guardian, please contact us at the email below and we will take appropriate steps.
