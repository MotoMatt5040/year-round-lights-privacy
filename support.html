Common questions

The app can't find my controller

Make sure your phone and the controller are on the same Wi-Fi network, and that the controller has finished its initial Bluetooth setup. Some Wi-Fi routers block device-to-device discovery ("client isolation" or "AP isolation") — if discovery fails, check that setting on your router.

Setup over Bluetooth isn't working

Confirm Bluetooth is enabled and that you've allowed the app's Bluetooth and Location permissions when prompted. iOS requires Location access only so it can read your current Wi-Fi network name to suggest it during setup — your geographic location is never read or stored.

My schedules didn't run

Schedules are stored on the controller itself and run even when your phone is off, but the controller must be powered on and connected to your Wi-Fi for them to fire. After adding or editing a schedule, leave the app open until you see it confirmed.
